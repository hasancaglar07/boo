## 🛠️ Implementasyon Guide

### DirectAnswerBlock Ekleme

**About Page Örneği:**
```tsx
<DirectAnswerBlock
  question="What is Book Generator?"
  answer="Book Generator is an AI-powered publishing studio that transforms your expertise into KDP-ready books in 30-90 minutes. Unlike general AI tools, it provides specialized book structure, consistent chapter generation, professional cover design, and EPUB/PDF outputs in a single workflow. Users include consultants, educators, and content creators worldwide."
/>
```

**Best Practices:**
1. Question natural language olsun
2. Answer 40-60 words
3. Self-contained content
4. Specific numbers/statistics ekle
5. Lead with answer, don't bury it

---

### Statistics Ekleme

**Format:**
```tsx
<p>According to our user survey, 92% of authors publish their books within 7 days of starting, compared to 3-6 months with traditional writing methods.</p>
```

**Best Practices:**
1. Specific sources cite et
2. Dates include et
3. Concrete numbers kullan
4. Verifiable claims yap

---

### Expert Quotes Ekleme

**Format:**
```tsx
<blockquote>
  "Book Generator represents the future of accessible publishing. It removes the technical barriers that stop most experts from becoming authors."
  <footer>— Jane Smith, KDP Publishing Consultant and Author of 5 Best-Sellers</footer>
</blockquote>
```

**Best Practices:**
1. Verifiable quote olsun
2. Expert credentials include et
3. Link to expert's website
4. Permission al
