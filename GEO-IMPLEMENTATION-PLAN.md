## 🚨 Risk Management

### Risk 1: Low Expert Quote Response Rate
**Mitigation:**
- Reach out to 20+ experts for 5 quotes
- Offer reciprocal promotion
- Provide value first (share their content)
- Consider paying for quotes if needed

### Risk 2: Wikipedia Page Rejection
**Mitigation:**
- Ensure notability requirements met
- Get press coverage first
- Use reliable sources only
- Consider hiring Wikipedia editor

### Risk 3: Low AI Citation Growth
**Mitigation:**
- Double-check DirectAnswerBlock quality
- Ensure statistics are verifiable
- Add more expert quotes
- Increase content production
- Improve third-party presence

### Risk 4: Resource Constraints
**Mitigation:**
- Prioritize high-impact pages first
- Use templates for faster content creation
- Consider hiring freelance writers
- Batch similar tasks together
