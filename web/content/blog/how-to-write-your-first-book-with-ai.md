## Common First-Time Author Mistakes to Avoid

### Mistake 1: Skipping the Outline Review
AI-generated outlines are excellent starting points, but you should review for:
- Logical flow
- Topic coverage
- Chapter balance
- Target reader alignment

### Mistake 2: Not Editing the Content
AI provides a strong foundation, but add your:
- Personal stories and examples
- Unique insights and experiences
- Professional expertise
- Voice and personality

### Mistake 3: Ignoring Cover Design
Your cover is the first thing potential readers see. Invest time in:
- Professional design
- Clear title readability
- Genre-appropriate styling
- High-resolution output

### Mistake 4: Not Testing Different Styles
Try different writing styles to find what works:
- Professional/academic
- Conversational/friendly
- Inspirational/motivational
- Technical/detailed
