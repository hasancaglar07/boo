## Step 2: Prepare KDP Metadata

### Title and Subtitle Strategy

**Title Best Practices:**
```
✅ Clear and descriptive
✅ Includes primary keywords
✅ 50-65 characters optimal
✅ No subtitle duplication
✅ Brand-aligned
```

**Examples:**
- "AI Book Writing: Complete Guide for Authors" (good)
- "The Ultimate Guide to Writing Books Using Artificial Intelligence Technology" (too long)

### Description Optimization

**KDP Description Formula:**
1. **Hook** - First 100 characters grab attention
2. **Benefit** - What reader will gain
3. **Authority** - Why you're qualified
4. **Keywords** - Natural keyword placement
5. **Call to Action** - Clear next step

**Example:**
```
"Transform your expertise into a published book in 30-90 minutes using AI-powered book production. This comprehensive guide shows you how to generate KDP-ready content, maintain quality control, and publish successfully. Based on real data from 92% of authors who published within 7 days. Perfect for consultants, educators, and content creators."
```

### Keyword Research

**KDP Keyword Strategy:**
```
1. Use 7 keyword slots maximum
2. Long-tail phrases convert better
3. Include competitor names for "vs" searches
4. Research Amazon search suggestions
5. Check keyword competition vs. demand

**Keyword Examples:**

## Advanced KDP Strategies

### KDP Select vs. Wide Distribution

**KDP Select (Exclusive):**
```
✅ KDP Unlimited (KU) inclusion
✅ 70% royalty on $0.99-$2.99 books
✅ Kindle Countdown Deals
✅ Free Book Promotions

❌ Cannot distribute elsewhere
❌ 90-day exclusivity requirement
```

**Wide Distribution:**
```
✅ Sell on multiple platforms (Kobo, Apple, Barnes & Noble)
✅ More control over pricing
✅ Diversified income streams

❌ No KU access
❌ 35% royalty on $0.99-$2.99 books
```

### Amazon Advertising

**Amazon Ads Strategy:**
1. **Start with Sponsored Products** ($5-10/day budget)
2. **Target competitor book names** ("vs" keywords)
3. **Test long-tail keywords** (lower cost, higher conversion)
4. **Monitor ACOS** (aim for under 30%)
5. **Scale winning ads** (pause losers)

### Series Strategy

1. Publish Book 1 (perma-free or $0.99)

5. Bundle books for higher value
```

## FAQ

**Q: Does KDP allow AI-generated content?**
A: Yes, KDP allows AI-generated content provided you own the copyright, meet quality standards, and don't mislead readers about AI involvement.

**Q: How long does KDP review take?**
A: KDP typically reviews books within 12-72 hours. Most books go live within 24 hours.

**Q: What royalty rate does KDP pay?**
A: KDP pays 70% royalties for books priced $2.99-$9.99, and 35% for books outside that range.

**Q: Should I enroll in KDP Select?**
A: If you want Kindle Unlimited access and promotional tools, yes. If you want wide distribution, no.

**Q: Can I update my book after publishing?**
A: Yes, you can update your book at any time. Changes take 24-72 hours to go live.
